A . P. J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, widely known as the "People's President" and the "Missile Man of India," served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born in 1931 in Tamil Nadu, Kalam was a brilliant scientist and engineer who played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. His immense contributions to space technology, particularly the successful Pokhran-II nuclear tests, earned him national acclaim. Beyond his scientific achievements, Kalam was a beloved figure for his inspirational speeches, humility, and dedication to education. His legacy continues to inspire millions, emphasizing the importance of knowledge, innovation, and selfless service.
